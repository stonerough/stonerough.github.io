Tara Brascamp

A counsellor and therapist since 1986, Tara runs workshops in Gestalt, sandplay and inner child work.

Tara sees life and work in a holistic way. She has lived in communities all over the world, experiencing the diversity of different cultures. From when she was a child she has always reached out to people she met on her journey. She has a deep commitment to spiritual growth work and for people to reach their fullest potential.

She has assisted with the running of a Training Group in Hamilton, for the Gestalt Institute of New Zealand. Tara established and then managed a "Special Needs" Preschool in Opotiki, before moving to Hamilton. She currently lives in Raglan.
